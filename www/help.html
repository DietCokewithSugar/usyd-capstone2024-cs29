Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

To create a custom tile design, go to the "Custom Design" tab and use our intuitive design tools to select patterns, colors, and sizes. You can save your design and order it directly from the app.
Once your order has been placed, you can make changes to it by contacting our customer support team within 24 hours. After this period, changes may not be possible depending on the production stage.
We accept a variety of payment options including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted to protect your information.
After placing your order, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order's progress on our website or through the shipping carrier's site.
You can contact our customer support team via email at support@tileapp.com or by calling +1 (800) 555-1234. Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.
