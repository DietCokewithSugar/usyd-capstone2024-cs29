About Us

Welcome to the Tiling App, a revolutionary user interface designed to streamline tile selection, layout design, and cost estimation for homeowners, contractors, and designers. Our app is part of RenoPilot's innovative solution to address the challenges faced in the global home improvement industry.

Founded in 2024, our mission is to empower users to make informed decisions when selecting materials and designs for tiling projects. Whether you're renovating your home or designing a commercial space, our app is here to help you every step of the way, providing instant cost estimates and visual previews within minutes.

Our Tiling App Features

  • Support for four layout types: Stack, Herringbone, Basketweave, and Lattice
  • Input surface dimensions and add obstacles
  • Instant cost estimates and visual previews
  • User-friendly interface for efficient design and estimation
  • Secure data storage in RenoPilot's Azure database
